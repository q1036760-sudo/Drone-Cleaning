The Challenge

Traditional Building Cleaning Is Overdue for Change

High-rise exterior maintenance has remained largely unchanged for decades — expensive, disruptive, and unnecessarily dangerous for workers.

Problem — Traditional scaffolding

Weeks of rigging, tens of thousands in equipment costs, and workers exposed to fatal fall hazards at height. Operations come to a standstill.

Problem — Rope access & abseiling

Requires expensive specialists, is weather-dependent, and carries significant liability exposure — limiting how often buildings can be cleaned.

Solution — Drone Cleaning Solutions

One operator on the ground. The drone reaches every surface. Ionized water cleans without chemicals. No scaffolding, no liability, no delays.
